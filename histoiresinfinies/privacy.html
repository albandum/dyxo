Histoires Infinies - Privacy policy

Privacy policy

Last update 2d June 2023

Histoires Infinies is an application that uses AI to generate stories by chosing a character, a place and an adventure. Histoires Infinies is a service provided by DYXO SAS.

We have written this policy to explain how Histoires Infinies ("we", "us" or "Histoires Infinies") uses the data you give us through our site(s), including but not limited to Histoires Infinies.

What personal data does Histoires Infinies collect via the Website?

Histoires Infinies collects different information depending on how you use the Website and how you interact with us. ‘Personal data’ means any data which can be associated with you as an individual, either directly or indirectly.

The personal data we collect via the Website is likely to include (and depend solely on the questions asked by each platform's administrators):

  • Identity information, such as your name, company name, organisation type and job title.
  • Contact data, such as your address, email address, telephone number.
  • Technical data, such as your internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform and other technology on the devices you use to access the Website.
  • Usage data, which includes information about how you use the Website and our services, and any communications we may receive from you.
Usage of said data
By providing personal information you are deemed to consent to your personal information being shared outside Histoires Infinies in the following circumstances:
  • where Histoires Infinies needs to share your information to provide the service you have requested.
  • where Histoires Infinies needs to send the information to third parties who work on behalf of Histoires Infinies to provide a service to you. We will only provide the information they need to deliver the service, and they are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.
  • where Histoires Infinies wants to keep you up to date on the latest service announcements, updates or other Histoires Infinies information
  • where you have given us permission to share your information with third parties
  • Histoires Infinies will also disclose your personal information if required to do so by law in urgent circumstances, to protect personal safety, the public or our website.
Data ownership

Histoires Infinies users that provide Application Question Responses:
Information you provide directly to an entity on Histoires Infinies that has created an application form in response to questions they have created specifically for their program or entity are called Application Question Responses. Application Question Responses that you provide to an entity on Histoires Infinies are available to that entity’s administrators and designated evaluators. Entities, to which you provide Application Question Responses may use that data, any associated basic information and data available by virtue of your relationship with that entity both on and outside of Histoires Infinies.

Histoires Infinies users that solicit projects to provide Application Question Responses:
Histoires Infinies will not distribute or otherwise make available any Application Question Responses information without your permission. This provision extends to any evaluation notes and custom tags created by Evaluators. You will be notified if Anonymized Application Question Responses information will be made available to a third party and you will have the opportunity to decline making such anonymized information available.
